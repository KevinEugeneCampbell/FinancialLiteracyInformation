Budgeting

Key Terms:

  • Fixed Expenses: expenses that stay the same from month to month, such as rent payments.
  • Flexible Expenses: expenses that change from month to month, such as how much you spend on utilties.
  • Total Expenses: total amount of fixed and flexible expenses.
  • Total Monthly Income: monthly income from your job or other resources.
  • Disposable Income: remaining income after subtracting income taxes.
Fixed Expenses:
  • Rent/Mortgage
  • Debt Payments
  • Insurance
  • Child Care
  • Subscriptions
  • Tuition
  • Home Internet/TV
Flexible Expenses:
  • Entertainment
  • Food/Groceries
  • Medical
  • Utilties
  • Personal Care
  • Gifts
  • Child Costs
  • Home Improvement/Maintenance
  • Pet Care
When calculating your budget you will need to calculate your Disposable Income then you will have to make a list of needs and wants with the appropriate prices for each item in the list. Using the amount you need to spend, subtract the total amount from your needs from your Dispoable Income. You also need to take into account how much you want to save per month and anything left over can be used on your wants, if you want.
