Financial Institutions

What are Financial Institutions? What do they do?
  • Provide financial products and servies to customers.
  • Offer secure storage for funds.
  • Lend money to borrowers and charge interest.
  • Invest reserved funds to generate income.
There are four types of Financial Institutions:
  • Commercial Banks
  • Credit Unions
  • Investment Banks
  • Insurance Companies
Benfits of Financial Institutions
  • Safety
  • Convenience
  • Access to credit
  • Investment opportunities
Why are Financial Institutions important?
  • Secure Transactions
  • Capital Facilitation
  • Economic Stability
  • Employment Generation
  • Wealth Services
  • Savings Promotion
  • Financial Literacy
  • Global Connectivity
If you cannot for some reason go to a bank/atm to check your account or make a transfer there is the option of Mobile Banking. By using Mobile Banking you can access your account from anywhere in the world with a internet connection. It is secure and cost-effective, talk to your bank for more information on mobile banking.
