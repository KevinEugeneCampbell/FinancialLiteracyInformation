Personal Finance

What is Personal Finance?

  • Personal Finance manages money for goals.
  • Involves goal setting, budgeting, saving, investing, asset protection.
  • Builds financial security and peace of mind.

There are 5 main areas of Personal Finance:
  • Income
  • Spending
  • Savings
  • Investing
  • Protection

The start to good personal finance is financial goals. These goals should be specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound.
